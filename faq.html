Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?
You can order by clicking the "Order via WhatsApp" button on any product card. This will open a direct chat with our sales team with your selected item pre-filled.
Do you offer delivery services?
Yes, we offer reliable delivery within Kampala and surrounding areas. Contact us via WhatsApp to arrange delivery for your specific order.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns on defective hardware within 7 days of purchase. Please ensure you have your receipt or order confirmation handy when reaching out to support.
Are these tools covered by a warranty?
Most of our professional-grade tools come with a manufacturer's warranty. Specific details vary by brand; please check the product description or ask our team.
